## 2. Inflection of verbs: different types

```text
1.
voida verbs (basic form ends in -da, -da)

voi/da   -n   voi   voinut   voidaan
saa/da   -n   sai   saanut   saadaan
vie/da   -n   vei   vienyt   viedaan
juo/da   -n   joi   juonut   juodaan
syo/da   -n   soi   syonyt   syodaan

Note:
kay/da   -n   kavi  kaynyt   kaydaan

2.
puhua verbs (basic form ends in two vowels)

puhu/a    -n      -i      -nut     -taan
luke/a    luen    luki    lukenut  luetaan
oppi/a    opin    oppi    oppinut  opitaan
naytta/a  naytan  naytti  nayttanyt naytetaan
osta/a    -n      osti    ostanut  ostetaan
anta/a    annan   antoi   antanut  annetaan
lenta/a   lennan  lensi   lentanyt lennetaan

Note:
tieta/a   tiedan  tiesi    tien/nyt, tieta/nyt  tiedetaan

3.
tulla verbs (basic form ends in two consonants + a, a)

tul/la    -en   -i   -lut  -laan
men/na    -en   -i   -nyt  -naan
sur/ra    -en   -i   -rut  -raan
nous/ta   -en   -i   -sut  -taan

Note:
ol/la     olen   oli   ollut   ollaan
3rd pers. on, ovat
juos/ta   juoksen juoksi juossut juostaan

4.
a) haluta verbs (basic forms ends in a vowel + ta, ta)

halu/ta   -an   -si   -nnut   -taan   halutkaa!

b) merkita verbs (most verbs in -ita, -ita)

merki/ta  -tsen  -tsi  -nnyt  -taan   merkitkaa!

c) paeta verbs (generally ending in -eta, -eta)

pae/ta    pakenen  pakeni  paennut  paetaan  paetkaa!

5.
Irregular verbs:

nah/da    nahen  naki  nahnyt  nahdaan
teh/da    teen   teki  tehnyt  tehdaan
```

### III. `k p t` changes in the inflection of verbs

`+ottaa` to take (representing `puhua` verbs)

`+mitata` to measure (representing `tulla`, `haluta`, and `paeta` verbs)

The strong grade is in italics.

Only the forms of `ottaa` are translated below.

```text
Affirmative                              Negative

Active Indicative

Present
Sing. 1. otan (mittaan)       I take      en ota (mittaa)        I do not take
	2. otat (mittaat)       you take    et ota (mittaa)        you do not take
	3. ottaa (mittaa)       he takes    ei ota (mittaa)        he does not take
Pl.   1. otamme (mittaamme)   we take     emme ota               we do not take
	2. otatte (mittaatte)   you take    ette ota               you do not take
	3. ottavat (mittaavat)  they take   eivat ota              they do not take

Past
Sing. 1. otin (mittasin)      I took      en ottanut (mitannut)  I did not take
	2. otit (mittasit)      you took    et ottanut (mitannut)  you did not take
	3. otti (mittasi)       he took     ei ottanut (mitannut)  he did not take
Pl.   1. otimme (mittasimme)  we took     emme ottaneet (mitanneet) we did not take
	2. otitte (mittasitte)  you took    ette ottaneet (mitanneet) you did not take
	3. ottivat (mittasivat) they took   eivat ottaneet (mitanneet) they did not take
```

```text
Perfect
Sing. 1. olen ottanut         I have taken      en ole ottanut        I have not taken
	2. olet ottanut         you have taken    et ole ottanut        you have not taken
	3. on ottanut           he has taken      ei ole ottanut        he has not taken
Pl.   1. olemme ottaneet      we have taken     emme ole ottaneet     we have not taken
	2. olette ottaneet      you have taken    ette ole ottaneet     you have not taken
	3. ovat ottaneet        they have taken   eivat ole ottaneet    they have not taken

Pluperfect
Sing. 1. olin ottanut         I had taken       en ollut ottanut      I had not taken
	2. olit ottanut         you had taken     et ollut ottanut      you had not taken
	3. oli ottanut          he had taken      ei ollut ottanut      he had not taken
Pl.   1. olimme ottaneet      we had taken      emme olleet ottaneet  we had not taken
	2. olitte ottaneet      you had taken     ette olleet ottaneet  you had not taken
	3. olivat ottaneet      they had taken    eivat olleet ottaneet they had not taken

Conditional Present
Sing. 1. ottaisin (mittaisin) I should take     en ottaisi (mittaisi) I should not take
	2. ottaisit (mittaisit) you would take    et ottaisi (mittaisi) you would not take
	3. ottaisi (mittaisi)   he would take     ei ottaisi             he would not take
Pl.   1. ottaisimme (mittaisimme) we should take emme ottaisi          we should not take
	2. ottaisitte (mittaisitte) you would take ette ottaisi          you would not take
	3. ottaisivat (mittaisivat) they would take eivat ottaisi        they would not take

Imperative
Sing. 2. ota (mittaa)         take!             ala ota (mittaa)      do not take!
Pl.   2. ottakaa (mitatkaa)   take!             alkaa ottako (mitatko) do not take!
	otetaan (mitataan)      let us take!      ei oteta (ei mitata)  let us not take!
	(= Passive Present)

Infinitives

3rd infinitive               ottamassa (mittaamassa) (in the process of) taking
				     ottamaan (mittaamaan) taking, to take

Participles
Past Active                  ottanut (mitannut) (one who has) taken
```
